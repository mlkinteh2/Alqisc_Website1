About Us

The AL-QIsC Background

ALQISC stands for Alqur'an and Islamic Club. Our aim is to encourage students to learn and teach the Holy Quran, inspired by the words of our Prophet (Sallallaahu 'alaihi wa sallam), who said, “The best of you are the ones who learn the Qur'an and teach it to others” [Al-Bukhari].
This club will help students recite, practice, and read the Holy Quran with proper tajweed and makhraj. Additionally, students will have the opportunity to practice Islamic arts and culture....
These opportunities will be available not only to students and staff but also to the wider community. We hope that this club will inspire us to practice Islam more diligently and benefit us both in this world and the hereafter.

Our Objectives

Mission and Vision

Mission

To promote Islamic values and teachings through meaningful activities by enhancing understanding of the Quran, empowering young Islamic leaders to serve their communities, fostering an inclusive and welcoming environment, and strengthening brotherhood and sisterhood through collaboration.

Vision

To become a distinguished platform for fostering Islamic education and community engagement, inspiring a deep love for the Quran and its teachings, and uniting a diverse Ummah through collaborative and spiritually enriching activities.

Guided by Aqidah, Akhlaq, Adab, Amanah, and Amalan principles, the club strives to nurture compassionate Islamic leaders dedicated to serving humanity.
